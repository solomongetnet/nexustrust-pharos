Introduction
Welcome to the Skill-to-Agent Dual Cascade Hackathon, a 6-week builder event launched by Pharos in partnership with Anvita Flow to celebrate Pharos’ 1st Anniversary.

Pharos is built to power the AI Agent economy, designed for on-chain payments, social interactions, and the deployment of intelligent agents at scale. This hackathon invites developers to help create the foundational layer of the Pharos AI Agent ecosystem by building reusable Skills and fully functional on-chain Agents.

The hackathon follows a dual-phase structure. In the first phase, developers will create Skills - reusable functional modules that AI agents can call to complete specific tasks. In the second phase, builders will combine these Skills to create complete Agents that can operate, transact, and interact on-chain.

With a total prize pool of 50,000 PROS, this hackathon is open to developers who want to build practical, scalable, and composable tools for the next generation of AI-powered on-chain applications.

Agent Arena — 25,000 PROS Awarded to selected Skill Hackathon winners who successfully build and deploy their Agents on Pharos.

Eligibility
The Skill-to-Agent Dual Cascade Hackathon is open to all developers interested in building for the Pharos AI Agent ecosystem.

Developers can participate individually or as teams. Participants are encouraged to build original, practical, and reusable components that can support future AI Agents on Pharos.

The Discord Skill Builder Campaign is designed for Pharos Discord community members, while the main Skill Hackathon is open to all developers through DoraHacks. The Agent Arena is designed for Phase 1 winners and selected open-entry participants who are ready to build complete Agents on top of Pharos.

Judging Criteria
Projects will be judged based on the quality, usefulness, and ecosystem relevance of the submitted Skills and Agents.

Key judging factors may include:

Originality and creativity of the idea
Technical quality and completeness
Practical use case for AI Agents
Reusability and composability of Skills
Successful deployment or integration on Pharos
User experience and clarity of documentation
Alignment with the Pharos AI Agent and on-chain economy vision
For Phase 1, judges will focus on the quality and usability of Skill modules. For Phase 2, judges will focus on complete Agents that can meaningfully operate, transact, or interact on-chain using those Skills.

About Us
Pharos is built to power the AI Agent economy through scalable on-chain infrastructure for payments, social interactions, and intelligent agent deployment.

As the AI and blockchain ecosystems continue to converge, Pharos aims to provide the infrastructure layer where agents can interact with users, execute tasks, move value, and participate in the on-chain economy.
